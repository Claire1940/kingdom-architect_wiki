<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Village Progression Route">

To turn a tiny starter plot into a massive medieval empire, you must follow a structured development path. Prioritize your economy before spending your hard-earned gold on expensive castle walls or cosmetic decorations.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build an Economic Base">
    Place your starter Houses and Markets close together near your spawn area. These structures generate the passive income needed to fund all future construction projects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish a Main Road Network">
    Lay down straight, clean roads connecting your buildings. Ensure at least one main road terminates directly at the edge of your current plot to prepare for future land expansion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Payouts and Reinvest">
    Regularly walk through your village to collect active building payouts. Immediately reinvest these funds into unlocking new structure tiers rather than buying immediate cosmetic upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase Adjacent Land">
    Once your starting area is fully optimized and running out of space, spend your accumulated gold to unlock the next adjacent plot of land.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fortify and Decorate">
    Link your new land to the existing road network. Begin placing defensive Towers, Castle Walls, and aesthetic elements like Gardens and Water features to complete the kingdom.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always complete your functional road grid before placing tiny decorative props. This ensures your villagers and buildings remain easily accessible.
</Callout>
