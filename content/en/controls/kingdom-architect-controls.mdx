The building interface allows you to select from several categories, including residential houses, commercial markets, infrastructure, and defensive fortifications. Proper alignment ensures you do not waste valuable land.

| Action Category | Primary Input / Control Function | Strategic Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Rotate Object** | Tap rotation hotkey / on-screen button | Align doors and paths with main roads |

| **Demolish / Move** | Select edit tool on active structure | Redesign layouts without losing progress |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Starter Progression Route">

If you want to grow your kingdom rapidly, you must follow a disciplined progression route. Avoid the temptation to spend your early gold on decorative trees or water features. Instead, focus entirely on your economic foundation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Village Core">
    Begin by placing your first Houses in a compact, organized block. These residential zones serve as your primary source of early-game progression and unlock points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Connect Commercial Markets">
    Place Markets adjacent to your residential zones. Markets generate higher active payouts, forming the core of your early-game cash flow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build a Clean Road Network">
    Use the infrastructure tools to lay down roads. Roads keep your village organized and make navigating your growing settlement much easier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect and Reinvest Payouts">
    Regularly click or tap on completed buildings to collect accumulated money. Reinvest every gold coin back into unlocking new structure categories.
  </Step>
</Steps>
