<Section icon={Target} title="Mobile UI Navigation & Building Setup">

The UI in Kingdom Architect is divided into several key zones that mobile players must master. At the bottom of the screen, you will find your primary navigation bar, which includes the Build Menu, Land Expansion, and Settings. Unlike the desktop version where hotkeys are available, mobile players must rely on quick taps to toggle between these menus during a busy building session.

The building process on mobile is streamlined into a multi-step touch sequence. Because screen real estate is limited, the build menu often occupies the bottom third of the screen, so it is vital to position your character in an open area before opening the menu to ensure you can see where you are placing your new house or market.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Build Menu">
    Tap the hammer icon at the bottom of the screen. This opens the category list, including Houses, Markets, Roads, and Towers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Structure">
    Scroll through the available buildings. Each card shows the cost and the progression value. Tap the building you wish to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Position and Rotate">
    A ghost image of the building will appear. Use one finger to drag it to the desired plot. Use the rotation button on the side of the screen to orient the front door toward a road.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Placement">
    Once the building turns green (indicating a valid spot), tap the green checkmark icon to finalize the construction and spend your gold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="UI Obstruction">
Be careful when building near the edges of your screen. The Roblox chat overlay or the mobile jump button can sometimes interfere with the "Confirm" button in the building menu.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Layout Ideas for Mobile Players">

Creating a beautiful kingdom on a touch screen can be challenging, but using specific design patterns can make the process much smoother. The "Fruit Street" layout is particularly popular because it uses a straight-line design that is very easy to navigate with mobile swipes. By keeping your commercial and residential areas separated by clear road networks, you reduce the risk of accidental taps and make your kingdom look professionally designed.

**Recommended Mobile Layouts:**
- **The Grid System**: Use straight roads to create square blocks. This is the easiest to manage on mobile because you don't have to deal with complex diagonal placements.
- **The Central Hub**: Place your most productive markets in the center of your plot, with houses radiating outwards. This minimizes the camera movement needed to collect your daily gold.
- **Waterfront Quarter**: Use the water and terrain tools to create a natural boundary on one side of your kingdom. This limits the directions you need to expand, making navigation simpler.

<Callout type="tip" title="Road Strategy">
Always build your roads first. It is much easier to fit a house into a pre-defined block than it is to try and weave a road through a cluttered mess of buildings on a small mobile screen.
</Callout>
