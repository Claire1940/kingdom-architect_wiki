<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Placing and Rotating Structures">

Creating a beautiful, high-yield city requires a systematic approach to building. Follow this step-by-step process to optimize your construction flow from a tiny settlement to a sprawling fortress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Core Road Network">
    Before placing any houses or markets, lay down a straight or gently curved main road. This serves as the anchor for all future building rotations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select and Rotate Your Building">
    Open your building menu and select a structure. Use the in-game rotation hotkeys (usually 'R' on keyboard or the rotate button on mobile UI) to align the front entrance with your road.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Grid Snapping and Boundaries">
    Hover the building over your desired plot. Ensure the footprint does not overlap with existing trees, water features, or neighboring building borders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Placement and Collect Payouts">
    Click to place the building. Once constructed, the building will immediately begin contributing to your progression and generating gold payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Overlap Issues">
If a building template appears red, it is overlapping with another object or extending past your purchased land boundary. Rotate the building or clear nearby obstacles to resolve this.
</Callout>

As you accumulate wealth, you can buy additional plots of land to expand your borders. Planning the layout of these new districts prevents your kingdom from becoming a cluttered mess.

### Popular Layout Archetypes

1. **The Compact Grid**: Best for pure gold farming. Align all houses and markets in back-to-back rows separated by single-lane roads.
2. **The Fortified Citadel**: Place your commercial markets in the center, surrounded by a ring of residential houses, with an outer perimeter of rotated castle walls and defensive towers.
3. **The Scenic Waterfront**: Shape rivers and ponds along the edges of your plot, placing decorative gardens and paths to create a realistic medieval village aesthetic.
