<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Route">

Transitioning from a small starter village to a massive castle city requires a structured plan. Follow this step-by-step progression route to maximize your build efficiency and maintain a steady flow of gold.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Village Area">
    Begin by placing basic Houses along a single straight road. Houses serve as your foundational residential structures, providing steady, reliable progression points. Keep this area compact to save room for commercial zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Set Up Commercial Markets">
    Once you have a few residential buildings, place Markets nearby. Markets act as commercial hubs and generate higher payout values. Position them close to your main road to simulate a realistic, bustling medieval town center.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect and Reinvest Payouts">
    Regularly walk through your active districts to collect accumulated gold from your buildings. Reinvest these early earnings directly into purchasing more Houses and Markets rather than buying cosmetic plants or fences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Advanced Building Categories">
    As your overall kingdom progression level rises, you will unlock new construction categories. These include defensive Towers, sturdy stone Walls, formal Gardens, and custom terrain modification tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Acquire New Land Plots">
    When your starting plot becomes crowded, spend your saved gold to purchase adjacent territory. Ensure your main road extends directly into the new plot before you begin constructing any new districts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Do not purchase land expansions too early. If you buy land before fully utilizing your current plot, you will waste valuable gold that could have been spent on income-generating buildings.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Maximize Money Farming">

Earning gold quickly is the key to unlocking the grandest castle components in Kingdom Architect. By optimizing your active play sessions and preparing your village for offline periods, you can keep your treasury overflowing.

### Active Farming Strategy
During active play, focus on a tight collection route. Walk a circular path through your residential and commercial zones to gather payouts as soon as they mature. 

### Passive Offline Strategy
Kingdom Architect features an offline growth mechanic that continues to generate progress and income while you are away from the game. Use this to your advantage by preparing your village before logging off.

| Preparation Step | Action Required | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **Maximize Plots** | Fill all empty starter spaces with basic Houses. | Increases the total passive payout pool. |
| **Upgrade Nodes** | Purchase any available economic upgrades. | Boosts the baseline value of offline rewards. |
| **Clear Queues** | Ensure no unfinished roads are blocking placements. | Prevents layout errors when you log back in. |
