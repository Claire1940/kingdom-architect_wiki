<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Starter Progression Route">

Transitioning from a tiny hamlet to a self-sustaining town requires a systematic approach. Follow these steps to optimize your build sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Economic Foundation">
    Begin by placing residential houses and market structures close together. Avoid scattering them randomly. Grouping these functional buildings allows you to collect payouts quickly without traversing large empty spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create a Simple Road Network">
    Lay down basic roads to define your town's shape. Roads are crucial for organizing future expansion. A single main road running through the center of your plot makes it easy to branch out later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect and Reinvest Payouts">
    Actively click completed building icons to collect payouts. Reinvest every gold coin immediately into purchasing more houses and markets. Do not save for cosmetic upgrades yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Next-Tier Structures">
    Once your starter area is filled, access the upgrade menu to unlock new building categories. This increases your income potential per square tile and prepares your economy for land expansion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Budget">
Do not buy expensive decorative assets like gardens, custom trees, or water features during your first 30 minutes. They drain your starting capital without providing any return on investment.
</Callout>

</Section>
