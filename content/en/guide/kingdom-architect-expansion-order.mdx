### Land Expansion Stages & Priorities

Each stage of your kingdom's growth demands a different spending priority. Use the table below to plan your budget and layout before purchasing your next plot of land.

| Stage | Main Goal | Recommended Land Use | Spending Priority | Planning Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Village** | Establish first settlement | Place initial houses, markets, and roads | Income buildings and early unlocks | Leave one side open for road connections |
| **First Expansion** | Open additional building space | Extend main road and place basic structures | Additional land and core upgrades | Avoid placing large decorations on borders |
| **Commercial Street** | Build a dedicated market district | Create a straight or curved vendor street | Markets, roads, and minor decorations | Use repeating spacing for easy extension |
| **Castle District** | Introduce defensive structures | Set up walls, towers, and courtyards | Castle walls, towers, and land expansion | Plan the outer wall before interior details |
| **Large Kingdom** | Connect all regional districts | Link residential, castle, and water zones | Advanced upgrades and final detailing | Complete functional layouts before landscaping |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Land Connection">
Never purchase a new plot of land unless you have enough remaining gold to immediately extend your main road into it. An isolated district cannot be easily integrated into your functional layout.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Land Progression Route">

To achieve maximum efficiency, you must follow a systematic progression path. This ensures that every coin spent on land acquisition translates directly into higher passive income.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the First Income Base">
    Fill your starter area with basic residential and commercial structures. Do not spend gold on trees, terrain modifications, or gardens yet. Focus entirely on maximizing your early building payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire the First Land Plot">
    Once your starter plot is filled, buy your first adjacent land plot. Immediately extend your primary road network into this new sector to establish a clear structural connection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Establish the Commercial District">
    Use this second plot to build a dedicated market district. Placing multiple markets along a single main road creates a high-yield commercial zone that dramatically boosts your passive income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Incorporate Castle Defenses">
    Purchase outer plots specifically for your castle district. Use this space to construct towers, defensive walls, and grand gatehouses to define the boundaries of your growing empire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize with Landscaping">
    With your core districts established and generating massive revenue, purchase the remaining surrounding plots to add natural beauty, including rivers, custom terrain, and dense forests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Redesigns">
Moving buildings costs time and resources. Plan your road network across multiple plots early to avoid having to clear out entire sections of your village when you unlock larger castle structures.
</Callout>

</Section>
