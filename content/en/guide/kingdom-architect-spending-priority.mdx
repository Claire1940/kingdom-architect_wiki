<Section icon={Map} title="Land Expansion and Layout">

Expanding your kingdom is a major milestone. However, purchasing land prematurely can drain your funds and leave you with empty, unproductive space.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Evaluate Capacity">
    Ensure your current land is at 90% building capacity before considering an expansion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan the Roadway">
    Extend your main road into the new area to ensure immediate connectivity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Designate Districts">
    Assign the new space a purpose (e.g., Commercial, Residential, or Castle) before placing any structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Build">
    Place income structures first, followed by defensive walls and towers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Expansion Warning">
Do not expand if you cannot afford at least three new high-tier buildings. An empty plot does not generate income and will slow your overall progress.
</Callout>

</Section>
