<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Kingdom Growth Route">

Transitioning from a quiet village to a sprawling castle city requires a structured approach. Following a step-by-step route prevents you from running out of gold or creating a cluttered layout that is difficult to redesign later.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Village Core">
    Begin by placing 4 to 6 houses and 2 markets in a compact grid. Connect them with a single straight road. This establishes your initial income loop without wasting precious space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Payout Collection">
    Actively walk through your village to collect payouts as soon as they become available. Use this immediate capital to purchase newly unlocked economic structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Your First Land Expansion">
    Once your starting plot is filled with functional buildings, purchase your first adjacent plot. Extend your main road directly into this new zone before placing any new buildings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Build a Dedicated Commercial Street">
    Use your newly acquired land to build a specialized market district. Grouping markets together creates a high-yield zone that is easy to harvest during active play sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Landscaping">
Avoid purchasing trees, gardens, water tiles, or terrain modifications during the first four steps. These cosmetic items consume gold without generating any financial return.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Maximizing Passive Offline Earnings">

One of the most powerful features in Kingdom Architect is the passive progression system. Your kingdom continues to generate wealth even when you are not actively playing. Understanding how to set up your town before logging off is key to returning to a massive treasury.

| Preparation Step | Action Required | Expected Outcome | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Clear Payouts** | Collect all active gold bubbles before exiting | Prevents caps on active generation | High |
| **2. Maximize Housing** | Fill all empty residential slots on your active plots | Boosts the baseline offline multiplier | Critical |
| **3. Upgrade Buildings** | Spend remaining gold on permanent structural upgrades | Increases the value of offline ticks | High |
| **4. Plan Next Plot** | Position your camera near the next plot you wish to buy | Quick transition upon logging back in | Low |

<Callout type="success" title="Offline Optimization">
Before logging out for the day, ensure every single available building slot on your unlocked plots is filled with either a house or a market. Leaving empty space on unlocked land directly reduces your potential offline yield.
</Callout>
