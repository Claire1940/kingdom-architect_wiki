<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Expansion Strategy">

Expanding your territory follows a specific loop of earning, unlocking, and building. To ensure you don't run out of gold or space, follow this verified progression route for **kingdom architect land expansion**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Economic Foundation">
    Start by filling your initial plot with basic Houses and Markets. These are your primary sources of gold. Do not worry about high-end decorations yet; focus on creating a stable payout loop that generates enough currency for your first land purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Essential Infrastructure">
    As your progress bar fills, you will unlock Roads and Paths. Before you buy new land, lay down a main road that leads toward the edge of your current plot. This ensures that when you do expand, the new territory is immediately accessible and ready for integration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Land Purchase">
    Navigate to the boundary of your kingdom and look for the expansion markers. The cost of land increases with each purchase, so always check your current balance against the required amount. Once purchased, the new plot will seamlessly join your existing territory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="District Zoning">
    Instead of building randomly, assign a "theme" to your new land. For example, use the second plot exclusively for a Commercial Street (as seen in the Fruit Street concept). This keeps your kingdom organized and makes it easier to manage payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reinvest Offline Earnings">
    If you find yourself short on gold for the next expansion, take advantage of the offline growth mechanic. Log out for a few hours, then return to collect the accumulated building payouts. This is often the fastest way to fund expensive late-game territory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Crowding">
Do not pack buildings too tightly at the borders of your land. Leave a small buffer zone for roads and walls, or you may find it impossible to connect your districts cleanly after expanding.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Growth and Offline Earnings">

Expansion is fueled by gold, and the most consistent way to earn gold is through the offline growth system. Kingdom Architect is designed to reward players who plan their kingdom and then allow it to "sim" over time.

Before you log off to let your earnings accumulate, perform a "Pre-Expansion Check":
1. **Collect All Payouts**: Ensure no building is sitting on a full reward.
2. **Max Out Current Space**: Place every available house and market you can afford.
3. **Check Upgrades**: Apply any kingdom-wide upgrades that increase building efficiency.
4. **Queue Land**: If you are close to the next expansion cost, wait a few minutes to buy it before logging off so you can plan the new layout in your head.

When you return, you will likely have a massive influx of gold. Use this specifically for **kingdom architect land expansion** rather than small decorations. Buying the land first gives you the "footprint" you need to build bigger and better.

<Callout type="tip" title="Return Strategy">
Upon returning to the game, collect your offline rewards and immediately look at the Land Expansion markers. If you can afford a new plot, buy it before spending gold on individual buildings. It's always better to have the space ready.
</Callout>
