<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Layout Construction">

Building a grand kingdom is a marathon, not a sprint. Follow these steps to ensure your **kingdom architect castle layout** evolves logically from a humble settlement to a majestic capital.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Village Core">
    Start by placing a cluster of **Houses and Markets** in your initial plot. Leave enough space between them for basic road paths. This area will serve as your primary income generator while you save for land expansion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define the Main Road">
    Build a straight or gently curved main road that runs from the center of your village toward the edge of your plot. This road will eventually connect your village to the castle gates in the next district.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand for Commercial Districts">
    Purchase your first land expansion and extend your main road. Use this new space to create a dedicated **Market Street**. Following a themed approach, like a "Fruit Street," helps organize your commercial buildings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Erect the Castle Walls">
    Once you have unlocked the third or fourth plot, begin placing **Walls and Towers**. Enclose a large area that will serve as the inner sanctum of your kingdom. This is where your most prestigious buildings will reside.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Integrate Natural Features">
    Use the **Terrain and Water** tools to add depth. A moat around your castle walls or a small forest at the village edge adds realism and variety to the layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Check">
By Step 3, your offline earnings should be significant. Always collect your payouts before starting Step 4 to ensure you have enough capital for expensive castle structures.
</Callout>

</Section>
