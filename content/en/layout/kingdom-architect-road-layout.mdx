<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Layout and Expansion Route">

Transitioning your starter village into a massive castle city requires a structured approach. Follow this progression route to scale your road network alongside your land expansions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Central Spine">
    Place a straight main road directly through the middle of your starting plot. Group your first houses and markets along this spine to establish a reliable, easy-to-collect income base.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create the Commercial District">
    As you unlock more markets, dedicate a specific branch of your road network to commerce. Space your vendor stalls evenly to allow room for minor decorative elements like trees and streetlights later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand and Connect">
    Purchase your first adjacent land plot. Immediately extend your central spine road into the new territory before placing any new buildings, ensuring the two zones are physically integrated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fortify the Boundaries">
    Once you have acquired 3-4 plots, outline your kingdom borders with defensive walls. Place towers at major road intersections and corner junctions to define your city districts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Your passive offline growth relies entirely on the efficiency of your active setup. A clean layout makes it easy to reinvest your offline earnings the moment you log back in.
</Callout>
