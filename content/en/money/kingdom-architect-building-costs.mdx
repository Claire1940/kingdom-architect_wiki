<Section icon={Target} title="Building and Progression Steps">

To minimize wasted resources, follow a structured approach to your kingdom's development. This progression path ensures you maximize your return on investment for every gold piece spent.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Foundation">
    Place your initial houses and markets in a compact area. Do not spread your buildings too thin, as this makes road management difficult and costly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Income Cycles">
    Collect payouts frequently. The faster you collect, the sooner you can reinvest in higher-tier structures that offer better yields.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand Strategically">
    Buy land only when you have a clear plan for it. Use the additional space to group commercial buildings together, creating a dedicated market district.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add Fortifications">
    Once your income is stable, introduce towers and walls. These structures are expensive but define the final look of your castle city.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Growth Strategy">
Focusing on income structures first allows your kingdom to scale naturally. Once you have a steady revenue, you can afford the more expensive castle walls and gardens.
</Callout>

</Section>
