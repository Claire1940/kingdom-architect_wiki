<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Maximizing Profits">

To build a truly great kingdom, you need a repeatable cycle of earning and reinvesting. The "Kingdom Architect income buildings" system rewards players who plan their growth in stages rather than building randomly across the map.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Village Core">
    Begin by placing a dense cluster of houses. Do not worry about aesthetics in the first five minutes; focus on building as many income-generating units as the starting plot allows. Leave a small gap for a central road that will eventually lead to your first expansion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate the Payout Loop">
    Buildings do not automatically deposit gold into your treasury in the early stages. You must actively monitor your settlement. As soon as a building indicates a payout is ready, collect it and immediately check if you have enough for the next structure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Commercial Markets">
    Once your residential base is stable, transition into building Markets. Markets generally have a higher cost but provide significantly better payouts. Positioning these near your main road creates a "Commercial Street" vibe that is both profitable and visually appealing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Upgrades">
    Use your accumulated wealth to purchase Kingdom Upgrades. These often increase the overall efficiency of your buildings or unlock higher-tier structures like the Stone Wall or Great Tower, which are necessary for the "Castle City" phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Growth Stage | Recommended Building Ratio | Target Income | Expansion Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Village** | 4 Houses : 1 Market | 500 Gold/Cycle | First Plot |
| **Town** | 8 Houses : 3 Markets | 2,500 Gold/Cycle | Commercial District |
| **Castle** | 15 Houses : 6 Markets | 10,000+ Gold/Cycle | Full Kingdom |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Over-Expansion">
Do not buy new land until your current plot is at least 70% filled with productive buildings. Buying land too early leaves you with a massive empty space and no income to fill it, slowing your overall progression.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering Offline Growth and Progress">

One of the most powerful features in Kingdom Architect is the ability to progress while you are away from your computer or mobile device. This "Offline Earnings" mechanic ensures that your kingdom continues to thrive even during your downtime.

**Key Offline Mechanics:**
- **Passive Accumulation**: Buildings generate a portion of their active income while you are logged out.
- **Preparation is Key**: The amount you earn offline is directly tied to the state of your kingdom when you last logged off.
- **Reinvestment**: Upon returning, your first action should be to collect the "Welcome Back" bonus and reinvest it into the next major upgrade.

| Feature | Active Play | Offline Progress |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Income Rate** | 100% Efficiency | Estimated 50-75% |
| **Collection** | Manual / Frequent | Bulk reward on login |
| **Building** | Real-time placement | No new construction |
| **Upgrades** | Immediate impact | Applied to next session |

<Callout type="info" title="Logout Checklist">
Before you exit the game, ensure all your buildings are placed and any available gold has been spent on upgrades. An empty plot generates zero offline income, so even a few low-tier houses are better than nothing.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Layout Ideas for Peak Efficiency">

While income is vital, the "Architect" part of the game's title shouldn't be ignored. A well-organized kingdom is easier to manage and expand. In 2026, players have developed several specialized layouts that balance "Kingdom Architect income buildings" with aesthetic appeal.

| Layout Name | Theme | Core Elements | Expansion Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Compact Village** | Efficiency | Grid-based houses, central market | Linear expansion |
| **Fruit Street** | Commercial | High-density markets, straight road | Decorative borders |
| **Walled Core** | Defensive | Central castle, outer residential | Radial expansion |
| **Waterfront** | Natural | River-side houses, bridges, trees | Shoreline growth |

**Layout Optimization Tips:**
1. **The Main Arterial Road**: Always build a single wide road from the center of your starter plot to the edge. This serves as the "spine" of your kingdom.
2. **Zoning**: Keep your Markets in a single row (Commercial District) and your Houses in another (Residential District). This makes collecting payouts much faster.
3. **Decorative Buffer**: Use Gardens and Trees only at the very end of your build or to fill small 1x1 gaps that cannot fit a house.

<Callout type="tip" title="Design Advice">
Follow the "Fruit Street Market" concept for your commercial area. By placing identical market stalls in a perfectly straight line along a decorated road, you create a high-income zone that looks professional and organized.
</Callout>
