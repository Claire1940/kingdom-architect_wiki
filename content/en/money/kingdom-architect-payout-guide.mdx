<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Offline Passive Earnings">

One of the most powerful features in Kingdom Architect is the passive offline growth mechanic. Your kingdom continues to work for you even when you are logged out of Roblox.

**Preparation Strategy Checklist:**
- **Fill all plots**: Ensure every available space on your unlocked land has a functional building.
- **Claim active payouts**: Collect all pending gold right before you log out of the game.
- **Purchase upgrades**: Spend your current balance on permanent kingdom upgrades to maximize offline rates.

| Offline Duration | Earning Efficiency | Best Reinvestment Action |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Short-term (1-2 Hours)** | 100% | Purchase immediate structural upgrades |
| **Medium-term (4-8 Hours)** | Stable | Buy new land plots to expand layout |
| **Long-term (12+ Hours)** | Maxed Cap | Fund expensive castle structures and walls |

<Callout type="info" title="System Info">
Offline earnings are calculated based on the total payout value of your placed structures. A kingdom packed with high-tier Markets will yield significantly more offline gold than one filled with decorative gardens.
</Callout>
