<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Spending & Upgrade Route">

Follow this optimized step-by-step route to manage your **kingdom architect upgrade costs** and avoid common financial bottlenecks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Starter Residential Zone">
    Place 4 to 6 basic Houses in a compact grid. Connect them with a simple, straight road. This setup ensures you can collect payouts quickly without walking long distances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish the Commercial Market">
    Construct your first Market near the entrance of your village. Markets have higher individual upgrade costs but offer superior silver yields compared to starter residential units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reinvest Payouts Into Upgrades">
    Collect every available payout manually. Reinvest 100% of these early earnings into upgrading your existing Houses and Markets rather than buying new land immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Advanced Structure Categories">
    Once your starter buildings reach their maximum efficiency, purchase the next tier of progression unlocks. This adds defensive walls, towers, and specialized decorations to your build menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute Controlled Land Expansion">
    Purchase your first adjacent plot. Extend your primary road network directly into this new zone and immediately establish a secondary residential or commercial district.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
By focusing on upgrading existing assets before expanding your physical footprint, you maintain a high income-per-tile ratio, which makes subsequent land purchases much easier to fund.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Maximizing Offline Earnings & Passive Growth">

Your kingdom does not sleep when you log out. The offline earnings mechanic is a vital tool for overcoming steep mid-to-late game upgrade costs. By preparing your kingdom correctly before leaving, you can return to a massive chest of silver.

| Preparation Step | Action Required | Impact on Offline Yield |
|---|---|---|
| **Maximize Active Plots** | Ensure all available space has functional buildings | **High** - Increases base generation rate |
| **Complete Pending Upgrades** | Spend current silver on building levels before logging out | **High** - Boosts passive multiplier |
| **Clear Construction Queues** | Ensure no half-finished blueprints are left on the field | **Medium** - Prevents blocked income tiles |
| **Plan Future Layouts** | Outline road extensions so you can build immediately on return | **Medium** - Saves active play time |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Offline progression calculated during your absence is capped based on your current kingdom level. Upgrading your core town infrastructure raises this cap, allowing you to store more passive income during longer offline periods.
</Callout>
