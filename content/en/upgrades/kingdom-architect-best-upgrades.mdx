<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Strategy">

To transform your starter plot into a massive castle city, you must follow a structured upgrade sequence. This ensures you never run out of gold while expanding your kingdom.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Village Core">
    Begin by placing basic residential structures. Focus on **Houses** and basic **Markets** to create a steady flow of passive income. Keep your layout compact to reserve space for future infrastructure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Implement a Road Network">
    Before purchasing your next plot of land, lay down a primary road. This acts as the spine of your kingdom, allowing you to seamlessly connect new districts without blocking future building zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Advanced Commercial Zones">
    Reinvest your accumulated payouts into higher-tier commercial buildings. Focus on specialized vendor areas to accelerate your gold-generation rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand and Fortify">
    Purchase adjacent land plots and establish your outer defenses. Use **Towers and Walls** to secure your territory and give your settlement a true medieval castle aesthetic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Road Connections">
Keeping one end of your main road completely clear of buildings makes it much easier to connect newly purchased land plots later on.
</Callout>
