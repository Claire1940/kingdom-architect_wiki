Expanding your kingdom from a single starter plot into a massive, interconnected medieval city requires careful planning. Follow this step-by-step progression route to optimize your land purchases and complete your **kingdom architect castle upgrades** efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Village">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Extend the Main Road Network">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Develop a Commercial High Street">
    Dedicate an entire land plot to a specialized market district. Align high-value vendor buildings along a straight street to create an organized town center.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Erect the Castle Fortifications">
    Once you have acquired a large, connected territory, plan your outer defensive perimeter. Place towers at the corners and link them with heavy stone walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Final Landscape Details">
    Use terrain tools, water paths, gardens, and trees to fill in the empty spaces between your residential, commercial, and castle districts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
