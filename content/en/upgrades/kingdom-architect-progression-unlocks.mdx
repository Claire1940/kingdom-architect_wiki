Revenue generation in Kingdom Architect continues even when you are not actively playing. This "offline growth" mechanic is crucial for reaching the most expensive **kingdom architect progression unlocks**. To optimize this, you must prepare your kingdom before logging off.

| Strategy Type | Action Item | Impact on Growth |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Active** | Rapid Payout Collection | High (Manual clicks required) |
| **Passive** | Offline Accumulation | Medium (Based on total buildings) |

**Best Practices for Offline Earnings:**
- **Upgrade First**: Spend your active session gold on upgrading the income rates of existing buildings.
- **Fill the Plots**: Never leave a purchased plot empty; even basic houses contribute to the offline total.
- **Collect Immediately**: Upon returning, collect all accumulated rewards first to fund your next building session.
