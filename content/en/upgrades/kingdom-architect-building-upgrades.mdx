<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Upgrade and Expansion Route">

Transitioning from a tiny settlement to a massive fortified castle requires a structured approach. Follow this step-by-step roadmap to optimize your progression speed and avoid running out of gold.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Village">
    Place your initial residential houses and basic markets in a compact grid. Ensure they are connected by a single main road so you can easily sweep through and collect payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Early Payouts">
    Actively collect completed payouts from your commercial buildings. Do not let finished cycles sit idle, as this halts your active income accumulation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Advanced Building Tiers">
    Reinvest your gold into progression upgrades that unlock high-tier residential structures. These upgraded buildings provide significantly higher payouts per square tile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Your First Land Expansion">
    Once your starter plot is fully optimized and crowded, purchase your first adjacent land plot. Extend your primary road directly into this new territory to keep the network unified.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Build a Dedicated Commercial District">
    Create a specialized market street (such as a themed Fruit Street vendor row) on your newly acquired land to concentrate your active gold generation in one easy-to-manage zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Construct the Castle Core">
    With a booming economy established, begin placing defensive towers, castle walls, and grand gate entrances to protect and define your central high-value districts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Expansion Warning">
Do not purchase new land if your current plot has empty space. Land costs scale up rapidly, and buying plots too early will starve your budget for actual building upgrades.
</Callout>

</Section>

As your kingdom grows, the cost of expanding your borders increases. You must plan your budget carefully around each major expansion milestone.

| Stage | Main Objective | Recommended Land Use | Spending Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter Village** | Establish first settlement | Compact residential grid | Income buildings & basic unlocks |
| **First Expansion** | Double building space | Extend roads, add more houses | Upgrading existing structures |
| **Commercial Street** | Maximize active gold | High-density market rows | Commercial upgrades & paths |
| **Castle Core** | Fortify central district | Towers, walls, and courtyards | Castle structures & gates |
| **Large Kingdom** | Connect all districts | Large-scale landscaping | Terrain, water, and decorations |

<Callout type="info" title="Planning Tip">

</Callout>
