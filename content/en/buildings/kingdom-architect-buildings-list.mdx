<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Kingdom Expansion">

Expanding your kingdom requires a balance of building placement, regular payout collection, and smart land purchases. Follow this structured progression route to scale your settlement efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Village">
    Begin on your starting plot by placing a compact cluster of houses and markets. Leave small gaps between them to accommodate future road connections.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish a Payout Collection Loop">
    Regularly walk through your active village to collect building payouts. This active cycle keeps your treasury full and ready for upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Your First Land Expansion">
    Once your starting plot is filled with income-producing structures, buy your first adjacent land expansion. Immediately extend your main road into this new territory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Build a Dedicated Commercial District">
    Use the newly acquired space to build a dedicated market street. Grouping commercial buildings together makes collecting payouts much faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fortify the Castle Core">
    Unlock towers and walls to construct a central fortified district. Use walls to define the boundary between the common village and the noble castle grounds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Offline Strategy">
Before logging out of Roblox, maximize the number of income-producing buildings on your plots. Kingdom Architect supports offline growth, meaning you will accumulate substantial gold while away, ready to be spent on land expansions when you return.
</Callout>

</Section>
