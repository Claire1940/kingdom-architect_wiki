<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Transitioning from a lone wanderer to the architect of a sprawling castle city requires a disciplined approach. Follow these steps to optimize your **kingdom architect houses and markets** placement and expansion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Village">
    Begin by placing 3-4 basic houses and 2 markets in a tight cluster. Do not worry about aesthetics yet; focus on generating enough gold to unlock the first land expansion. Collect payouts every time the icon appears above the building.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Develop the Commercial Street">
    Once you purchase your first additional plot, extend a main road from your village. Line this road with markets to create a "Commercial Street." This concentrated zone makes gold collection much more efficient and mimics the "Fruit Street" layout seen in high-level player builds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Advanced Infrastructure">
    As your income grows, use the "Upgrades" menu to unlock towers, walls, and specialized decorations. At this stage, you should have at least 10 houses and 5 markets active to support the high cost of castle-tier components.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize the Castle Core">
    Reserve your largest land plots for the castle district. Use walls to encircle a central courtyard featuring gardens and water features. Keep your houses and markets outside the main walls to simulate a realistic medieval citadel layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Over-Decoration">
It is tempting to spend all your gold on trees and terrain early on. However, these provide zero income. Ensure your houses and markets are fully optimized before spending more than 10% of your budget on beautification.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Land Expansion and Layout Strategies">

Expanding your kingdom is not just about buying more space; it is about how you use that space to connect your **kingdom architect houses and markets**. A poorly planned layout will lead to "dead zones" that are difficult to reach or aesthetically displeasing.

| Expansion Stage | Main Objective | Recommended Land Use | Spending Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Plot** | Survival | High-density housing/markets | Income buildings |
| **First Expansion** | Connectivity | Main road extension | Roads and more markets |
| **Town Phase** | Zoning | Separate residential/commercial | Upgrades and paths |
| **Citadel Phase** | Fortification | Outer walls and towers | Castle structures |
| **Empire Phase** | Landscaping | Rivers, forests, and gardens | Terrain and decoration |

<Callout type="info" title="The Grid vs. Organic Layout">
While a grid layout is the most efficient for maximizing building count, an organic layout with curved roads and varied building orientations creates a more "fantasy" feel. Most top-tier architects use a hybrid approach: grid-based markets for efficiency and organic housing for aesthetics.
</Callout>

When planning your layout, consider the "District" method. Assign specific land plots for specific purposes:
- **The Harbor/Waterfront**: Use water and terrain tools to create a coastal trading hub filled with markets.
- **The Forest Edge**: Place houses among clusters of trees for a secluded, rustic atmosphere.
- **The High Street**: A straight, wide road lined with your highest-earning commercial buildings.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Maximizing Offline Earnings">

One of the most powerful features in Kingdom Architect is the ability to grow while you are not actively playing. Your **kingdom architect houses and markets** continue to accumulate value based on their tier and placement.

To make the most of your time away, ensure your kingdom is "primed" before you log out:
1. **Clear all Payouts**: Ensure no building is sitting on a completed cycle.
2. **Max Out Capacity**: Place as many income-generating buildings as your current land and budget allow.
3. **Queue Upgrades**: If you have the funds, purchase the next progression unlock so it is ready for you upon your return.
