<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Fortification Placement">

Building a cohesive defense network requires a systematic approach. If you place walls too early, you risk blocking your expansion routes and forcing expensive demolitions. Follow this proven progression sequence to integrate walls and towers seamlessly into your kingdom layout.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Economic Core">
    Establish your residential houses and commercial markets in a compact central grid. Ensure this starter area is generating a steady stream of passive payouts before investing in non-income structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Map Out the Main Roads">
    Lay down your primary road network. Roads should extend from your town center to the edges of your current land plot, leaving open pathways for future expansion zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Anchor the Corners with Towers">
    Identify the key intersections and outer corners of your planned district. Place your first towers at these locations to serve as structural anchors for your impending wall segments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Connect the Wall Segments">
    Draw your walls between the established towers. Ensure the walls run parallel to your roads, leaving clear gaps for functional gatehouses and pedestrian pathways.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Redesign Warning">
Always leave at least one side of your walled district open or fitted with an easily removable wall segment. This ensures you can seamlessly connect new land plots when you purchase expansions.
</Callout>

</Section>
