<Section icon={Coins} title="How to Complete Early Decoration Quests">

When starting out, completing quests is the fastest way to earn coins. The game features specific milestones that require you to place environmental elements to progress your kingdom's development.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Place Basic Rocks">
    Access your build menu and navigate to the decorations tab. Select the basic rock prop and place at least 5 of them around your initial peasant huts to complete the early cosmetic milestone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Expand with Land Tiles">
    Purchase a new land tile to expand your buildable territory. This unlocks more building space and allows you to plan dedicated garden zones away from your noisy industrial structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Lay Down Pathways">
    Select the path tool and place at least 10 path tiles connecting your peasant huts to the apple stall and granary. This satisfies the quest requirements and earns you a substantial coin boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish Advanced Gardens">
    Use your quest rewards to purchase additional decorative items. Placing five more decorations will complete the secondary aesthetic questline, setting you up for the automated tax collector phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Budget Management">
Avoid spending all your early-game coins on premium decorations. Secure a steady income of at least 7 to 10 residential houses before investing heavily in expensive garden layouts.
</Callout>

To maximize your kingdom's rating, you must design layouts that balance functional buildings with beautiful green spaces. Here are three highly effective layout templates.

| Layout Style | Residential Ratio | Garden/Decor Ratio | Best Suited For | Prestige Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Urban Grid** | 70% | 30% | Rapid coin farming, compact spaces | Average (★★★☆☆) |
| **Garden Suburb** | 50% | 50% | Balanced progression, high visual appeal | Excellent (★★★★★) |
| **Industrial Hub** | 80% | 20% | Granary and market optimization | Decent (★★★★☆) |

### Design Best Practices
- **Symmetry**: Align your pathways in clean grids, but break up the straight lines with circular garden plazas in the center of residential areas.
- **Color Coordination**: Use the recolor tool to match the roofs of your cottages with the surrounding flower beds and trees.
- **Functional Spacing**: Always ensure your peasant huts have their front doors facing a designated pathway to maintain realistic citizen movement.
